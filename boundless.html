Welcome to the page for our Minecraft Boundless mod!

But what is the Boundless Mod?

The Boundless mod for Minecraft 1.12.2 is a mod where you can explore the endless void looking for the rarest of minerals, travel the far lands of the known Minecraftian Earth looking for a fabled temple that vibrates in conjunction with the Earth. Look for rare loot, or try to manufacture it yourself. Of course, you *can* do this all.
